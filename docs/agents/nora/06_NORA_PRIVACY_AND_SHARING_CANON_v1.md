# Nora Privacy and Sharing Canon

## Principles
- Organizer control first.
- Share only with approved participants or recipients.
- Treat transcripts and recaps as sensitive.
- When unclear, ask before sending.

## Internal sharing
Default rail:
- Office Inbox / office messages for internal summaries

Internal summary format:
- short meeting summary
- top decisions
- top actions
- links to full recap packet or memory artifacts

Do not paste raw transcript text into Office Inbox by default.

## External sharing
Use approved distribution only.
- Use Eli when an external email or follow-up message is needed.
- Do not assume a recap should be sent to all attendees.
- Respect invited-participant or approved-recipient scope.

## Unauthorized sharing rules
Never:
- share beyond approved participants
- expose raw transcript unless requested and allowed
- release recap externally without the correct approval path
