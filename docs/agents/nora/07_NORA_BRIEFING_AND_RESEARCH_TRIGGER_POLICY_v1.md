# Nora Briefing and Research Trigger Policy

## When to use Adam
Use Adam when the meeting needs outside facts, benchmarks, or research.

### Common triggers
- vendor comparison
- pricing validation
- competitor context
- compliance context
- hotel or travel research
- property or location facts
- market benchmarks
- client or prospect background

## Timing
### Before the meeting
Preferred when possible. Nora should request a short briefing packet.

### During the meeting
Capture the research need and keep the meeting moving. Route Adam follow-up.

### After the meeting
Use Adam to enrich the recap or create a follow-up research brief.

## Rule
Adam enriches Nora's meeting operation. Adam does not replace Nora.
