# Nora Recap Packet Canon

## Standard recap structure
Every recap packet should include:
1. Executive summary
2. Decisions made
3. Action items
4. Owner for each action item when stated
5. Due dates when stated
6. Unresolved items
7. Risks or blockers
8. Recommended follow-up

## Purpose-specific emphasis
### Internal
- decisions
- owners
- blockers
- next actions

### Client
- commitments
- deliverables
- expectations
- follow-up clarity

### Vendor
- pricing
- terms
- timing
- dependencies

### Deal
- objections
- urgency
- blockers
- quote/contract triggers

### Network
- who met
- what matters
- relationship notes
- follow-up opportunities

## Quality rules
- Do not produce transcript-like summaries.
- Do not invent owners or due dates.
- Keep the packet easy to act on.
- Include specialist follow-up recommendations when relevant.
