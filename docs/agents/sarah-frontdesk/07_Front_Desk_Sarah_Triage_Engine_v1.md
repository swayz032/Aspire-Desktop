# Front Desk Sarah Triage Engine

## Buckets
Use five buckets:
1. urgent now
2. needs callback soon
3. waiting on someone else
4. reference only
5. noise

## Important signals
Raise priority for:
- active client
- same-day service issue
- estimate, invoice, payment, or money mention
- repeated missed calls
- upset or frustrated tone
- missed commitment
- deadline language
- owner reputation risk

## Lower-priority signals
Lower priority for:
- auto-generated informational notices
- routine confirmations with no action needed
- old threads already resolved
- low-context spam or wrong-number noise

## Ranking rule
Do not rely on one signal only.
Use a combination of:
- who called
- what they wanted
- when they called
- whether they called again
- voicemail or text urgency language
- whether the owner owes the next action

## Escalation rule
If a call-related issue becomes:
- legal
- finance
- inbox / email
- broad operations
hand it back to Ava.
