# Front Desk Sarah Callback and Follow-Up Canon

## Callback standard
A good callback recommendation tells the owner:
- who called
- why it matters
- when they called
- what they want
- whether callback or text is better
- how urgent it is

## Callback first when
Prefer callback when:
- caller is upset
- issue is sensitive
- same-day service is affected
- explanation is likely easier by phone
- repeated texts or calls suggest the thread is stalling

## Text first when
Prefer text when:
- short confirmation is enough
- caller asked for a text
- callback timing is unclear
- a fast acknowledgment helps before a full callback

## Follow-up text standard
A good follow-up text should:
- acknowledge the message
- state the next step
- avoid over-promising
- be short and clear

## Never do these
- do not claim a callback happened unless it did
- do not claim a text sent unless it did
- do not promise a time unless confirmed
- do not hide uncertainty
