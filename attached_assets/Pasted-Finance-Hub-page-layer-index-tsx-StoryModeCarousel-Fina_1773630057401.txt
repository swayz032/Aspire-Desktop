Quality Bar (applies to every item above)
Enterprise-grade polish: consistent 16-24px padding on all cards, no layout jank, smooth 300-500ms transitions, no clipped or overflowing content.
Readable at a glance: all label text minimum 11px with sufficient contrast (white/60 on dark), all value/heading text minimum 15px in white or bright accent. No gray-on-gray text anywhere.
Charts legible: chart axis labels at least 10px and visible (not clipped), trend lines use the mode accent color at full opacity, data points or bars clearly differentiated, no overlapping text.
Premium card treatment: every card uses #1C1C1E or #0A0A0F background, 1px border at rgba(255,255,255,0.07), 14-16px border-radius. No glow effects whatsoever — no box-shadow with accent colors, no neon border halos, no radial glow bleed around card edges. Cards are clean, sharp, and professional. The only visual depth comes from the dark border and the card background contrast against the page.
Typographic hierarchy: section labels uppercase 10-11px/muted, sub-labels 12-13px/muted, primary values 24-40px/bold/white, body text 13px/white-60. No font sizes outside these ranges.
Spacing consistency: 12px gap between cards in the same row, 20px gap between rows, 16-24px internal card padding — uniform throughout the page.
